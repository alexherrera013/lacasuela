La Casuela Mexican Restaurant & Bar is located in the heart of Downtown Ripon.

Located in Main Street at the corner of Stockton Avenue. Across from Barnwood Arms in the heart of Ripon.

From Modesto, Take Hwy 99 to Ripon, Take Ripon exit (Main St). At the off-ramp stop continue straight on the overpass, take a right on Stockton Avenue, either turn Right on Main (We are located on 107 E. Main St) or Stay on Stockton Ave, pass Main St and turn right onto the rear parking located on the Right hand side of Stockton avenue.

From Manteca or Stockton, take Hwy 99 South to Ripon; take Ripon exit (Main St). At the off-ramp stop make a right down the overpass, make a right on Stockton Avenue, either turn Right on Main (We are located on 107 E. Main St) or Stay on Stockton Ave, pass Main St turn right onto the rear parking located on the Right hand side of Stockton avenue.
